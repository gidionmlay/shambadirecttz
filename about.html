Shamba Direct

Empowering smallholder farmers with modern agro-mechanization tools, services, and financing.

What We Do

Shamba Direct is a digital platform that helps smallholder and medium-scale farmers—especially youth and women—access modern agro-mechanization tools, services, and financing. Using USSD, WhatsApp, and Web interfaces, farmers can register, discover equipment, apply for credit, and track deliveries, all without leaving their communities.

Our Mission

To democratize access to quality agro-equipment and financing for smallholder farmers—fostering productivity, sustainability, and prosperity from Soil to Success.

Our Vision

A future where every farmer—regardless of location or capital—has the tools, knowledge, and financing they need to thrive.

Our Story

Shamba Direct was founded in 2024 in Arusha by Imani Penza to address the daily challenges faced by rural farmers. Inspired by the transformative power of accessible tools and financing, Imani created a mobile-first platform that bridges gaps between farmers, suppliers, and financiers.

What began as a pilot with 100 farmers and a handful of suppliers quickly grew into a national solution. Today, Shamba Direct partners with verified suppliers and financial institutions, serving thousands of farmers under programs like YEFFA by AGRA Tanzania.
