Seasonal Farming Tips

October Planting Guide

It's time to prepare your land for the short rains. Consider these high-yield crops for this season:

  • Beans - Plant early in the month
  • Maize - Select drought-resistant varieties
  • Green vegetables - Kale and spinach perform well

Pest Management Tips

As the rainy season approaches, be prepared to manage these common pests:

  • Fall armyworm - Monitor maize crops closely
  • Aphids - Use natural repellents for vegetables
  • Bean flies - Early treatment is essential
